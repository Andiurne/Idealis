# Arteries
An organization of luciant, farming monks that operates all over Halisan. Because dyed gimynfire can contaminate foodstuffs, it is very dangerous to ever mix the two, and so many societies struggle to develop farming technology that either exceptionally secures dyed gimynfire from leaking or doesn't rely on it. The Kuthjïuman solution to this problem, in accordance with their land-revering culture, is to have an entirely distinct caste of luciant farmers, who forswear gimyndine and train their bodies to supramortal levels in order to serve the land.   

In order to fulfill their purpose, the Arteries practice a wide range of activities. They train in martial arts, both to discipline their bodies and to better fend off Shades encroaching on village settlements; they also train in meditation, in order to build their own soulfire and practice handling it without the assistance of 
engrams.  

Given their peculiar status, the group is treated like a social caste entirely separate from the normal hierarchy, being simultaneously valued and ignored. Mostly, common practice is to thank them for their service, and repay them by protecting their land and elsewise leaving them alone.

## Individual Roles
### Cleanser
Like the public defenders of shade combat. Usually, those that are particularly powerful will be able to find more gainful employment in becoming mercenaries, so those that remain are either fairly principled or fairly low in soulflow.

However, veteran cleansers will often offer basic combat lessons to those willing, and many a master combatant can trace their instruction back to a cleanser.

### Cultivator
Simply, the lifeblood of any arterial nation. Cultivators are more or less solely responsible for staple food production, including planting, maintenance, accelerating growth with harmonic infusion, and harvesting.

## Pulmonaries
A pulmonary is an outpost of Arteries, typically serving it's community in some combination of shade defense, food supply, health, and various traditional ceremonies.

### The Core
The largest pulmonary, which is led by the Lifeheart themselves; currently the position is filled by [Alfiürwit](Narratives/Characters/Birth%20of%20a%20Nation/Alfiürwit.md).

It sits, appropriately, at the "heart" of the Arenfürich rainforest, in the outlands of Raulseách. With growth planes stretching for acres, and some of the most talented cultivators in the corps, the Core produces nearly all of Raulseách's food supply by itself, and ships excess along each of the outgoing rivers.
